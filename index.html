Welcome to the People's Democratic Republic of Goetzman

A playful, affectionate parody honoring Michael Goetzman: hacker, conference-builder (SecretCon and CypherCon), solarpunk enjoyer, and original DEF CON staff legend.

Citizens of the internet are hereby informed that Supreme Treasurer Goetzman now presides over an economy backed entirely by collectible comedy prop bills.

  • National bird: the packet-sniffing falcon.
  • Official energy policy: solar panels on every hackerspace roof.
  • Primary export: extremely niche cybersecurity jokes.
  • Secondary export: conference lore and suspiciously shiny badges.

Treasury notice: “In Goetzman We Trust” notes are legal tender only at imaginary embassies, CTF afterparties, and among friends who appreciate a good roast.
